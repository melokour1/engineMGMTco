FAQs

Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between SEO and Google Ads?
Google Ads puts you at the top of results for as long as you keep paying. The moment the budget runs out, your visibility disappears. SEO (what we do) earns your ranking organically — Google places you there because it considers you the most relevant result. Once you're there, you stay without ongoing ad spend.
Which companies do you work with?
We work exclusively with local businesses — auto shops, dentists, plumbers, lawyers, restaurants, home services, anything where customers search for your service plus a city or neighborhood. If people search "your service near me" we can help.
How much time will I need to invest?
Very little. We handle the work on our end. During onboarding we'll need about 15 minutes to get secure access to your Google Business Profile. After that, we send you regular updates so you always know where you stand.
What are the contract terms?
Month-to-month. No long-term lock-in. You can cancel anytime. Most clients stay because the results speak for themselves, but we never force anyone to stick around.
